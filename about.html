About

City Chapels is a Funeral Home that was born from the desire to provide Filipinos with what they deserve after losing a loved one: attentive, compassionate and professional customer service that will ultimately allow them to create the perfect tribute and celebrate a life.

Excellence

Coordinator available 24/7 for your family.

Family-Centered

Family-friendly, comfy chapels with amenities.

Personalized Care

Catering to individual needs and preferences.

Employee Value

Empowering employees to deliver exceptional service.

Mission

To provide exceptional, personalized care for families, fostering a compassionate and supportive environment where dignity and well-being are paramount.

Vision

To be the leading provider of family-centered services, renowned for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and the positive impact we make on the lives of our clients and communities.
